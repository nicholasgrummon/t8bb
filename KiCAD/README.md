# KiCAD Schematics
I originally designed and assembled t8bb in January 2022 as a hobby project in college (just in my free time, not for credit).<br>
I revisited the project in Fall 2024 after graduating to consider upgrading the computer into a more robust PCB kit that I could potentially sell.
Here I have committed my KiCAD schematics and PCB layouts for the intended upgrade.<br>
However, the upgrade did not come to pass at the time because the supplier I ended up ordering from applied the SMD LEDs backwards.<br>
Not realizing this, I burned out the LEDs on the first board I powered up. I had ordered a handful and tried to desolder and reorient the other LEDs but ruined the boards.
I tried to place several more smaller orders with the same supplier, but they reversed the polarity of the LEDs each time.
So I had to give up on making PCBs for the time.<br>
